RMTS Industries

RMTS Industries was commissioned by the Student Rocket Program (SRP) to design, build, test, and deliver a complete Rocket Motor Test Stand (RMTS). This system is designed to meet customer needs and provides a safe, reliable, and standardized ground testing platform for rocket engines, fully supporting aerospace engineering and scientific research activities. RMTS includes all subsystems and supporting equipment required for system operation and maintenance, and provides complete technical support, operation manuals and system training services to ensure that users can conduct testing work independently, safely and efficiently.
